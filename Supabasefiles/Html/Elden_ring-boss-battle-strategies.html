Boss-Battle-Strategies

Your complete guide to defeating every major boss in Elden Ring

Conquer every boss in Elden Ring with this extensive strategy guide. From early-game field bosses to late-game demigods, this guide breaks down attack patterns, recommended levels, elemental weaknesses, status effects, and the best tactics for both melee and ranged builds. Whether you're struggling with aggressive foes or massive arena-wide attacks, these strategies will help you stay alive—and strike back harder.

Boss Name Location Recommended Level Key Strategies Notable Rewards
Tree Sentinel Limgrave – Church of Elleh Road Level 15–20 Use Torrent for mobility, bait shield-bash attacks, and strike after his gallop swings. Weak to lightning damage. Golden Halberd, Sentinel Armor (later)
Margit, the Fell Omen Stormveil Castle Gate Level 20–25 Learn his delayed combos, roll toward his staff attacks, and use Spirit Ashes to create pressure. Bleed builds perform well. Talisman Pouch
Godrick the Grafted Stormveil Castle – Chapel Level 30–35 Stay close to avoid wide axe sweeps; punish his recovery after tornado slams. Watch for fire attacks in phase two. Great Rune of Godrick, Remembrance of the Grafted
Radahn, Scourge of the Stars Caelid – Redmane Castle Level 50–60 Summon festival allies, dodge comet arrows with sideways rolls, and punish his recovery after gravity attacks. Stay mounted for mobility. Radahn’s Great Rune, Remembrance of the Starscourge
Malenia, Blade of Miquella Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree Level 120+ Avoid trading hits—she heals on every successful strike. Learn the timing for Waterfowl Dance, use frost or bleed for high damage, and stay patient. Remembrance of the Rot Goddess
