Master every charm, curse, and utility spell in Hogwarts Legacy

Learn every spell available in Hogwarts Legacy, from essential classroom charms to powerful combat-focused curses. This guide shows you exactly where and how to learn each spell, the quests required, and the most effective ways to use them across exploration, puzzles, and battles. Whether you want to uncover hidden rooms, master dueling combos, or enhance stealth gameplay, this guide provides every detail you need.

Spell List & Unlock Requirements

Spell Type How to Learn Best Uses
Accio Control Learned during the first Charms Class with Professor Ronen. Pull enemies closer, move puzzle objects, start combo attacks.
Levioso Control Learned in Defense Against the Dark Arts Class. Lift enemies, interrupt attacks, solve platforming and puzzle mechanics.
Incendio Damage Unlocked after completing Professor Hecat’s first assignment. Close-range fire damage, lighting torches, burning obstacles.
Depulso Control Earned by completing Professor Sharp’s assignment. Push enemies away, activate push-based puzzle mechanisms.
Expelliarmus Damage / Disarm Granted after completing Professor Hecat’s second assignment. Disarm enemy wizards, drop weapons from goblins, trigger combo openings.
Glacius Control Learned through Madam Kogawa’s first flying lesson assignment. Freeze enemies, set up shattering combos for bonus damage.
Bombarda Damage Unlocked via Professor Howin’s assignment. High-damage explosions, breaking large structures, crowd control.
Revelio Utility Learned in the opening tutorial with Professor Fig. Reveals items, hidden passages, loot, and puzzle clues.
Confringo Damage Reward for completing Sebastian Sallow’s questline early stages. Long-range fire blasts; excellent for mid–long distance combat.
Disillusionment Stealth Learned during the main quest “Secrets of the Restricted Section.” Stealth exploration, ambush attacks, sneaking through guarded areas.
Alohomora Utility Unlocked in “The Caretaker’s Lunar Lament.” Upgrades require Demiguise Moons. Unlocking doors, accessing hidden rooms, collecting rare loot.
Crucio Unforgivable Curse Learned if you choose to accept Sebastian’s lesson in his questline. High single-target damage, curse debuffs for combo amplification.
Imperio Unforgivable Curse Available during Sebastian’s quests if the player agrees. Turns enemies into temporary allies in combat.
Avada Kedavra Unforgivable Curse Unlocked in Sebastian’s late-game quest if you accept the spell. Instantly defeats most enemies; devastating in cursed enemy combos.

How to Build Effective Spell Combos

Unlocking spells is only half the journey—mastering spell rotations turns you into a top-tier duelist. Try combining control, damage, and utility spells to maximize efficiency in battle.

  • Levioso → Accio → Confringo: Classic air juggle that keeps enemies helpless.
  • Glacius → Bombarda: Shatter enemies for bonus damage.
  • Disillusionment → Petrificus Totalus: Stealth one-hits for clearing forts.
  • Crucio → Imperio → Avada Kedavra: Full curse synergy for massive wipe potential.
